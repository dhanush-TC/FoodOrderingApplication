Delicious Breakfast Menu

Masala Dosa

 

Price-- rs.99

A South Indian delight, masala dosa is a thin, crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potato.

Ingredients: Rice and lentil batter, potatoes, spices, oil.

Instructions: Spread batter on a hot griddle, add potato filling, fold and serve.

Poha

 

Price-- rs.149

Poha is a light and flavorful dish made from flattened rice, often cooked with spices and vegetables.

Ingredients: Flattened rice, mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, turmeric.

Instructions: Wash poha, temper spices, add vegetables, cook and serve.

Upma

 

Price-- rs.129

Upma is a savory dish made from semolina and vegetables, perfect for a hearty breakfast.

Ingredients: Semolina, mustard seeds, chilies, ginger, vegetables.

Instructions: Roast semolina, temper spices, cook with vegetables, and serve hot.
