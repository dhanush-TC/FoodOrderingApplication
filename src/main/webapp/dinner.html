Tempting Dinner Menu

Butter Chicken

 

Price-- rs.369

A rich and creamy curry made with succulent pieces of chicken in a tomato and butter-based sauce.

Ingredients: Chicken, tomatoes, butter, cream, spices.

Instructions: Marinate chicken, cook in sauce, add cream, and serve with naan or rice.

Paneer Tikka

 

Price-- rs.229

Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in spices and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients: Paneer, yogurt, spices, bell peppers, onions.

Instructions: Marinate paneer and veggies, grill, and serve as a starter or main.

Vegetable Biryani

 

Price-- rs.299

A fragrant rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, vegetables, and sometimes with meat or paneer.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, vegetables, spices, saffron, nuts.

Instructions: Cook rice and vegetables with spices, layer, and steam to perfection.
